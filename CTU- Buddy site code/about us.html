Our Vision

Our vision is a future where students don't have to stress about the quality of the information that they receive, because we want to make it available from a single reliable source. Gone are the days where students have to stress about whether or not their assignments were received, not knowing what campus events are taking place and last not being able to voice their problems

Our System's Purpose

As a group we have discussed the importance of a quality website that makes the communication between student and faculty seamless. And that is what we aimed to do with this website

Our Mission

Our mission is simple, we aim to improve faculty and student relations by incorporating healthy communication habits.
